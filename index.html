About Me

Hi! I am currently a PhD student at the State Key Laboratory of Intelligent Technology and Systems, Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Fuchun Sun. I obtained my bachelor degree from Department of Computer Science, Tsinghua University in 2020.
I am broadly interested in research on the Reinforcement Learning and Robotics.
